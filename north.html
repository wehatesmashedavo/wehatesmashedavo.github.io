Manchester's Best Brunch

North Manchester


North Manchester has seen a surge in popularity recently, with lots of brunch places opening. Check out the best places below!


Redbank
Redbank is tucked away in the Green Quarter, and definitely worth the short trip out of town to try. They also host pop ups and food events so keep your eyes peeled for some unmissable food.


Black Milk
Black Milk is home of the ultimate filthy breakfasts. Think bowls of cereal covered with chocolates, or milkshakes with a whole slice of cheesecake on top. Phwoar!


Kettlebell Kitchen
Kettlebell Kitchen is a fitness lovers dream, with the whole menu designed to be high in protein to help you meet your fitness goals. From lean fry up's to protein waffles, you're in for a treat here.
